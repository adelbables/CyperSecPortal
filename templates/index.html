Stenography

Our stenography tool is an innovative way to communicate securely and protect your privacy. It allows you to encode a message into an image, which can then be decoded by anyone who knows that the image encrypted is and has an account for our website. This is a great way to share sensitive information without worrying about it being intercepted or compromised. Simply type your message into the text box and click "Encrypt" to create a stenography image. To decode the message, go to the decrypt page than simply upload your encrypted image and click "Decrypt".
